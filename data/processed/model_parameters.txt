=== COMPARTMENTAL MODEL STRUCTURE (Annex 1) ===
6 compartments: S (susceptible), E (exposed/latent),
  I_R (infectious, will recover), I_D (infectious, will die),
  R (recovered), D (deceased)
SEIR-type ODE model, homogeneous mixing, deterministic (mean-field)
Exponential sojourn times (Markov property)
No population structure, no adaptive behaviour, no interventions
Post-mortem transmission captured via I_D compartment

=== ODE SYSTEM ===
dS/dt = -beta * S * (I_R + I_D) / N
dE/dt =  beta * S * (I_R + I_D) / N - sigma * E
dI_R/dt = (1 - p_death) * sigma * E - gamma * I_R
dI_D/dt = p_death * sigma * E - mu * I_D
dR/dt =  gamma * I_R
dD/dt =  mu * I_D

beta = R0 * gamma  (when mu = gamma; general: R0 = beta * E[T_inf])
  where E[T_inf] = (1 - p_death)/gamma + p_death/mu

=== PARAMETER VALUES (Table A, Annex 3) ===
Population size N: 13,392,200
  Source: McCabe et al. [6] (Ituri + North Kivu)
  Distribution: fixed

R0 (basic reproduction number): 1.24
  Source: Mean of estimates in Choi et al. [16]
  Distribution: normal (for MC sampling)

CFR / infection fatality proportion (p_death): 0.32 - 0.54
  Source: Lower bound MacNeil et al. [17], upper bound Rosello et al. [18]
  Distribution: uniform (sampled from this range)

Incubation period (1/sigma): 10 days (range 2-21)
  Source: CDC [15]
  Distribution: gamma-based with specified mean and bounds
  (time from infection to becoming infectious)

Presymptomatic period: 6.22 days
  Source: Velasquez et al. [14]
  (time until first 'dry' symptoms appear; fixed)
  Alpha (fraction of incubation with dry symptoms) = (10 - 6.22) / 10 = 0.378

Infectious period when recovering (1/gamma): 10 days (range 2-26)
  Source: Wamala et al. [19]
  Distribution: gamma-based with specified mean and bounds

Infectious period when dying (1/mu): 10 days (range 3-21)
  Source: Wamala et al. [19]
  Distribution: gamma-based with specified mean and bounds

Reported deaths at 22 May 2026: 204 (suspected)
  Source: INSP [8] and McCabe et al. [6]

Total infections by 22 May 2026: 1,054
  Source: McCabe et al. [6] (back-calculation from 204 suspected deaths)

Dark factor deaths (under-ascertainment): 0.806
  (fraction of deaths not reported; estimated from suspected deaths
   and total cases as of 22 May 2026)

=== MODEL INITIALISATION (t=0 = 18 May 2026) ===
D(0) = 131 (initial suspected deaths from INSP)
R(0) = D(0) * (1 - p_death) / p_death
x_init = 677 (initial total infected: I_R(0) + I_D(0) + D(0) + R(0))
  Derived from ratio 1054/204 * D(0)
I_R(0) = (x_init - D(0) - R(0)) * (1 - p_death)
I_D(0) = (x_init - D(0) - R(0)) * p_death
E(0) = (1 / ((1-p_death)*sigma / (gamma + lambda1) + p_death*sigma / (mu + lambda1))) * I(0)
  where I(0) = I_R(0) + I_D(0), lambda1 = largest real eigenvalue of linearised system

=== IMPORTATION STEP (Annex 2) ===
Per-traveller infection probability on day t:
  p(t) = E(t)/N * (1 - alpha * p_E) + I(t)/N * (1 - p_I)

  where:
    p_E = 0.1  (travel reduction for dry symptoms; 10% lower than susceptible)
    p_I = 0.9  (travel reduction for wet symptoms; 90% lower than susceptible)
    alpha = 0.378  (fraction of incubation period with dry symptoms)
    E(t) = exposed count from compartmental model
    I(t) = I_R(t) + I_D(t) = infectious count from compartmental model
    N = 13,392,200

Daily importation probability (N_out travellers on day t):
  p_imp(t) = 1 - (1 - p(t))^{N_out(t)}

Cumulative importation probability over study period (t=1..15, i.e. 11-25 June):
  p_imp = 1 - PRODUCT_{t=1}^{15} (1 - p_imp(t))

Travellers per importation (day t):
  N_out,pI = 1 / p(t)

=== TRAVEL PROBABILITY REDUCTION PARAMETERS ===
p_E (dry symptoms): 0.1
  Source: ECDC expert opinion (mild symptoms unspecific)
p_I (wet symptoms): 0.9
  Source: ECDC expert opinion (infectious individuals too sick to travel)
