B. WHO Disease Outbreak News (DON) — primary public-health-source statements
================================================================================
- Source     : https://www.who.int/emergencies/disease-outbreak-news/
- Format     : HTML (WHO does not serve DONs as PDF)
- Saved to   : data/raw/who_don/
- Files      :
    * DON602.html  — 15 May 2026 — DRC initial declaration
                     (246 suspected cases, 80 deaths, 4 confirmed deaths)
    * DON603.html  — 21 May 2026 — DRC update
                     (83 confirmed, 9 deaths, CFR 11%; Uganda 2 conf, 1 death)
    * DON605.html  — 29 May 2026 — DRC + Uganda update
                     (DRC 125 conf, 17 deaths, CFR 14%; Uganda 9 conf, 1 death)
    * DON606.html  —  8 June 2026 — DRC + Uganda update
                     (DRC 515 conf, 91 deaths, CFR 17%; Uganda 19 conf, 2 deaths)
- Licence    : WHO public-content terms; cite as WHO DON602 / DON603 / etc.
- Note       : The plan's pre-stated "8 cases / 4 deaths on 15 May" was wrong.
               The INRB data shows 8 CUMULATIVE on 14 May (the first SitRep day),
               but the WHO DON602 official statement reports 246 suspected / 80
               total deaths (4 among confirmed) as of 15 May. We use the INRB
               time series as authoritative and cross-check with the DONs.

DATA EXCLUSIONS (explicitly NOT used)
================================================================================
- "837 cases / 196 deaths on 17 June 2026" from the original draft plan —
  this figure IS now verified via the INSP_SitRep time series
  (837 / 196 on 15 June, 896 / 232 on 17 June). The "17 June" date in the
  draft was off by 2 days; the figures themselves are correct.
- "CFR 25–51%" — original plan invention. Real CFR is 11–18% among confirmed
  cases per WHO DONs (lower than historical Bundibugyo outbreaks due to
  early supportive care; includes backlog-confirmed cases).
